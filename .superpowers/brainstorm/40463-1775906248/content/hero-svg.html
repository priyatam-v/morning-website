Hero SVG — Which approach?

The current decorative SVG should feel like a preview of Morning. Here are three interpretations — all using your existing palette and SVG-only.

A — Single Card Preview

One realistic knowledge card, full detail: category strip, illustration area, hook text lines, footer actions. Feels like you're actually looking at the app.

B — Card Stack (Fanned)

Three cards fanned behind each other, each with a different category color strip. Implies a collection, a daily habit. The depth suggests "more where this came from."

C — Minimal Card + Category Dots

A single card outline showing all five category colors as dots. Quieter — more suggestive than literal. Matches the brand's restraint, but may not communicate "app" clearly enough.
